Conclusions¶

At this point, our TMDb movie data analysis project is done. There are few limitations on this analysis:

  • There is serious lacking of foreign country movie data on the dataset. Our analytical result will be more precise if movie data from same movies showed on other countries.
  • It should also include movie data such as popularity, and revenue on foreign cinemas.

Base on this project, we can conclude the following:

  • Movie industry has production bloom since the 90's. Movie genres like drama, comedy, thriller, action, and romance has the highest production rate.
  • The production rate of movie genre, Drama, spiked up tremendously around the 2010.
  • The top 20 favorite movie in U.S. are Avatar, Titanic, Jurassic World, and The Avengers etc.
  • Most of the top 20 movies are mainly action, fantasy, thriller, and adventure movie genres. We can conclude these genres usually have a promising revenue.
